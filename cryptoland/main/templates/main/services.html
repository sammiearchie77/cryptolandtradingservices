Bitcoin Escrow Service

An escrow service is a mediator service that keeps the money for a transaction in safekeeping until the Bitcoins are handed over. Escrow protects buyers from fraudulent sellers by requiring the Bitcoin to be deposited up front, before any money changes hands

Bitcoin Mining

Bitcoin mining is the process of creating new bitcoin by solving a computational puzzle. Bitcoin mining is necessary to maintain the ledger of transactions upon which bitcoin is based. Miners have become very sophisticated over the last several years using complex machinery to speed up mining operations.

Bitcoin Transaction

Bitcoin (BTC) was created to function as peer-to-peer electronic cash. ... Bitcoin transactions are messages, like email, which are digitally signed using cryptography and sent to the entire Bitcoin Network for verification. Transactions are public and can be found on the digital ledger known as the blockchain.
